Our Process

AI-Powered Cross-Stacking
Technology

We don't depend on a single data source. Our proprietary cross-stacking workflow pulls from multiple premium platforms and verifies every data point through a multi-layer pipeline.

01

ICP Deep Dive

We work with you to define the exact profile of your ideal customer — job titles, industries, company size, geography, and buying triggers. No generic lists.

02

AI-Powered Discovery

Using Clay's waterfall enrichment engine combined with Apollo's 275M+ contact database, we identify and extract every prospect matching your ICP across multiple data sources simultaneously.

03

Cross-Stack Enrichment

Each prospect is enriched through our cross-stacking pipeline — pulling phone numbers, emails, company data, and job details from Lusha, Apollo, and additional premium providers to maximize accuracy.

04

Multi-Layer Verification

Every phone number is verified through Twilio's carrier-level lookup API to confirm it's active, identify mobile vs. landline, and flag any disconnected numbers. Then scrubbed against the National DNC Registry for full compliance.

05

Delivery

Clean, verified leads delivered in your preferred format — CSV, direct to your CRM, or through our client dashboard. Every lead is a real person, with a real number, at a real company.
