Why It Matters

The Real Cost of Inaccurate Data

Wasted Sales Time

When 40% of your lead list has bad phone data, a sales rep making 60 calls per day wastes 24 of those dials. At an average fully-loaded SDR cost, that's thousands of dollars per month per rep spent on dead ends instead of revenue-generating conversations.

Damaged Team Morale

Nothing kills a sales team's energy faster than an afternoon of disconnected numbers and wrong contacts. High-performing reps leave organizations that give them bad tools. The cost of replacing a trained SDR far exceeds the cost of better data.

Missed Revenue Windows

Decision-makers don't wait. The VP evaluating your competitor's solution right now won't be in-market next month. When your data is wrong, you miss the window entirely. By the time your team reaches the right contact, the deal has already been decided — and you weren't in the conversation.

Compliance Risk

Calling numbers on the DNC list or outside legal calling hours carries real financial penalties. The FTC can fine up to $50,120 per violation. A single unscrubbed lead list could expose your organization to significant legal liability that no lead vendor will indemnify you against.
