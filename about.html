Cross-Stacking Technology

Multiple Sources. One Truth.

Cross-stacking is our approach to data enrichment. Instead of trusting a single provider's data, we query multiple premium databases for every single lead and use the agreement between sources to determine the most accurate information.

01

Waterfall Enrichment

For every prospect, we cascade through multiple data providers in sequence. If Provider A doesn't have a direct phone number, Provider B is queried. Then Provider C. This continues until the highest-confidence data point is found. Coverage rates that would be 40% from a single source jump to 80%+ through waterfall enrichment.

02

Cross-Reference Validation

When multiple independent data sources agree on a contact's phone number, the confidence level rises dramatically. We track which providers confirm each data point and weight our confidence accordingly. A number confirmed by three independent sources is far more reliable than one found in a single database.

03

Carrier-Level Verification

After enrichment, every phone number passes through carrier-level API verification. This confirms the number is currently active, identifies whether it's a mobile or landline, detects the carrier network, and flags any numbers that have been recently disconnected or ported. This is the final quality gate before any lead reaches your team.

04

Continuous Data Refresh

Contact data decays at roughly 30% per year. People change jobs, switch phone numbers, and move companies. Our pipeline isn't a one-time pull — we continuously refresh and re-verify data to ensure that what we deliver reflects reality, not a snapshot from six months ago.
